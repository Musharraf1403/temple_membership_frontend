Temple's New Home

We are raising funds to construct a new temple for the Liverpool Murugan Temple, established in 2017. After operating in a rented space for 7 years, we have secured our own land and are now working towards building a beautiful, traditional temple to serve the Hindu community in Wirral.

Your support will help create a sacred space for worship, cultural events, and community gatherings. Join us in this noble cause and contribute to bringing peace, prosperity, and blessings to all.
